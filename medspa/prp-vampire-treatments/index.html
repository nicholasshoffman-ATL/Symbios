What are Vampire Face Procedures (PRP Treatments)?

Our PRP-based treatments use your body’s own regenerative properties to restore and revitalize skin. Options include:

Symbios Signature Vampire Facial

Combines microneedling with PRP to stimulate collagen, improve texture, and reveal a natural, youthful glow.

Symbios Signature Vampire Facelift

Non-surgical procedure using PRP with hyaluronic acid fillers to restore facial volume, smooth wrinkles, and improve skin tone while enhancing facial contours.

These treatments work with your body’s natural healing mechanisms to rejuvenate skin, reverse signs of aging, and provide long-lasting results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Vampire face procedures painful?

Most clients experience minimal discomfort; topical numbing is applied for comfort.

How long do sessions take?

Treatments typically last 60–90 minutes depending on the procedure.

Is there downtime?

Most patients experience mild redness or swelling for 24–48 hours but can return to normal activities immediately.

How many treatments are needed?

Your customized plan depends on your goals, but many see noticeable improvements after one to two sessions.
