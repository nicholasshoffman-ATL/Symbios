Smooth Wrinkles, Restore Volume, and Refresh Your Appearance

Aging, environmental stressors, and natural changes in skin volume can leave the face showing fine lines, wrinkles, and loss of youthful contours. Non-surgical injectables and dermal fillers provide a safe, effective way to rejuvenate your appearance while maintaining a natural look.

At MedSpa by Symbios in Hilton Head Island, we offer Botox, Xeomin, and Juvéderm, along with customized dermal filler treatments to restore facial volume, enhance contours, and soften wrinkles.

What Are Injectables & Fillers?

Our treatments are designed to address common signs of aging and restore natural beauty:

  • Botox & Xeomin: FDA-approved injectables that temporarily relax targeted facial muscles, softening fine lines and wrinkles for a smoother, refreshed appearance.
  • Juvéderm Dermal Fillers: Restore lost volume, enhance facial contours, and smooth deeper wrinkles in areas like cheeks, lips, nasolabial folds, and marionette lines.
  • PRP-Enhanced Fillers: In select cases, platelet-rich plasma can be combined with fillers to promote skin regeneration, improve texture, and support longer-lasting results.

Who Can Benefit from Injectables & Fillers?

Injectables and fillers are ideal if you want to:

  • Smooth wrinkles and fine lines
  • Restore facial volume lost with age
  • Enhance lip fullness or cheek definition
  • Improve facial symmetry and contour
  • Rejuvenate skin without surgery
