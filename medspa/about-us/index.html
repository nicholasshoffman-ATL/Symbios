About Us

Who We Are

At Symbios® Health, we believe true wellness goes beyond just treating symptoms—it’s about building healthier habits that elevate your everyday life. Our unique, integrated health and wellness campus brings together primary care, fitness, nutrition, and aesthetics—all in one connected location—to support your health from every angle.

Whether you’re here for preventive care, personalized training, hormone optimization, skin rejuvenation, or nutritional support, our expert team collaborates to create a plan that’s tailored to you.

The Symbios Health campus is more than a place—it’s a community dedicated to personalized, holistic care. We focus on measurable results and sustainable change, guiding you through each stage of behavioral transformation—from awareness to lasting action.

Meet the Experts

Our MedSpa Team

Dr. Stephen Luther

Founder

Dr. Stephen Luther is the founder of Symbios Health and the lead aesthetic and regenerative physician at MedSpa by Symbios Health. With over 20 years of clinical experience, he combines medical precision with an artistic eye to deliver safe, natural-looking results. Dr. Luther personally performs all injectables and regenerative treatments. He is the region’s only Vampire Facial® and Vampire Facelift® provider, for which he was trained and certified by Dr. Charles Runels.

Victoria Blintser

VP of Regenerative Health and Wellness

Victoria brings a thoughtful, client-centered approach to aesthetics and wellness. Her experience includes helping lead a Miami medical spa that was among the first to introduce laser hair removal in the city in the late 1990s.

As Vice President of Regenerative Health and Wellness at Symbios Health, she helps lead the growth and development of ARIA and FIT, with a focus on team empowerment, service excellence, and meaningful client experiences. She is passionate about building an environment where people feel welcome, respected, and well cared for throughout their journey.

Saida Razmetova, LE

Regenerative Health Skin Specialist

Saida takes a science-driven approach to skin health, focused on building strong, resilient skin and supporting long-term function. Her work is guided by an intentional treatment “velocity” that prioritizes collagen stimulation and barrier integrity.

Aligned with Symbios’ integrative philosophy, she views skin as a reflection of internal and external factors—including nutrition, hormones, lifestyle, and targeted skincare—delivering results that are both visible and lasting.

Born and raised in the former USSR, she brings a disciplined, education-driven perspective to her work. She holds a Bachelor’s degree in International Journalism and Linguistics and has built a loyal clientele.

Emily Gill, RN, BSN

Nurse

Emily Gill, RN, BSN, is an experienced nurse with over 11 years in healthcare and 7 years in nursing practice. She specializes in advanced aesthetic treatments, including laser procedures with the Alma Hybrid and PRP hair restoration. Emily is known for her clinical precision, compassionate care, and commitment to natural results, creating a supportive environment where clients feel confident and understood.
