Our Services

Our Body Contouring & Treatments

Accent Prime® Skin Tightening & Body Contouring

Using advanced ultrasound and radiofrequency technology, Accent Prime® reduces stubborn fat, tightens loose skin, and redefines body contours—without surgery, pain, or downtime. Safe for the face and body, treatments deliver firmer, smoother skin with visible, lasting results.

BeautiFill Laser Liposuction

A minimally invasive option that combines gentle laser liposuction with fat transfer, BeautiFill sculpts a more balanced shape while using your own fat to enhance volume in targeted areas. Achieve natural-looking results with long-lasting effects.

HydraFacial Body Treatments

Extend the benefits of HydraFacial to the body. These treatments deeply cleanse, exfoliate, and hydrate areas such as the back, arms, and décolletage, leaving skin smoother, clearer, and refreshed.

Body-Specific Chemical Peels

Target rough texture, uneven tone, and breakouts on the back, chest, or arms with professional-grade chemical peels. Reveal brighter, smoother skin while promoting long-term renewal.

Laser Acne & Scar Treatments

Address acne and scarring at the source with advanced laser therapy. Reduce inflammation, eliminate acne-causing bacteria, and support skin healing for clearer, healthier skin on the face and body.
