Facials & Skin Rejuvenation Treatments

Our comprehensive facial treatments are designed to improve skin health, texture, and tone while addressing your specific concerns. Options include:

Symbios Signature Facial

Our customized facial treatment blends cleansing, exfoliation, hydration, and targeted serums to leave your skin glowing, balanced, and refreshed. Ideal for all skin types and tailored to your specific concerns.

HydraFacial

This multi-step treatment deeply cleanses, exfoliates, and infuses the skin with hydrating serums for an instantly smoother, brighter, and more radiant complexion—no downtime required.

Chemical Peels

Chemical peels gently remove dull surface layers to reveal smoother, more even-toned skin. Great for improving texture, fine lines, acne, and hyperpigmentation with customizable intensity.

Dermaplaning

This non-invasive exfoliation technique removes dead skin cells and fine facial hair (“peach fuzz”), enhancing skin texture and allowing for better product absorption and smoother makeup application.

Microneedling

Microneedling stimulates collagen production by creating micro-injuries in the skin, improving the appearance of fine lines, scars, and overall skin texture with minimal downtime.

ClearLift Lips

A gentle yet powerful laser treatment that targets fine lines around the lips, boosts collagen production, and enhances natural lip definition for a smoother, more youthful look.

ClearLift Skin Restoration

This advanced, non-ablative laser therapy penetrates below the skin’s surface to lift, firm, and rejuvenate without pain or downtime—ideal for treating fine lines, pigmentation, and dullness.

Scar, Acne, and Skin Rejuvenation

Target scarring, acne marks, and uneven skin tone with personalized treatments that promote healing, smooth skin texture, and restore clarity using advanced skin-renewing technologies.
