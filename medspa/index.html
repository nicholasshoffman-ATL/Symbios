Welcome

Where Science Meets Aesthetic Transformation

Welcome to MedSpa by Symbios Health, the premier medical spa in Hilton Head Island. Unlike ordinary spas, our MedSpa combines medical expertise with artistic precision, delivering regenerative aesthetics, anti-aging treatments, and advanced skin rejuvenation—all under the personal care of Dr. Stephen Luther.

Dr. Luther, widely recognized for his compassionate care as a physician, brings decades of medical experience to Vampire Facelifts (PRP treatments), and injectable artistry. As the only provider in the region personally certified by Dr. Charles Runels, the creator of the Vampire Facial and Vampire Facelift, Dr. Luther is uniquely qualified to offer results-driven aesthetic procedures that enhance natural beauty while supporting regenerative health.
