What is Alma Duo® for Erectile Dysfunction?

The Alma Duo® is a minimally invasive, in-office therapy that uses low-intensity shockwave and ultrasound technology to stimulate blood flow in the penis. By addressing the underlying vascular causes of ED, it provides a natural, long-term solution rather than merely masking symptoms.

  • Quick 15-minute in-office treatment
  • No anesthesia or topical numbing required
  • Painless procedure with no downtime
  • Safe, drug-free, and non-surgical

How Alma Duo® Works for Erectile Dysfunction

Alma Duo® delivers gentle, low-intensity pulses to the penis, stimulating new blood vessel growth, improving circulation, and promoting natural tissue regeneration. The painless treatment is performed in a private, comfortable setting, with many men noticing improvements within a few weeks that continue over time. Benefits of treatment include:

  • Improves vascular function: Low-intensity pulses stimulate blood flow for stronger, firmer erections
  • Long-lasting results: Clinically validated effects lasting up to two years after treatment
  • Convenient and private: Six painless sessions completed over 2–3 weeks
  • No interruptions: Enjoy spontaneous intimacy with no recovery period

Who Can Benefit from Alma Duo®?

Alma Duo® is ideal for men experiencing:

  • Occasional or chronic erectile dysfunction
  • Difficulty achieving or maintaining erections
  • Reduced sexual confidence or satisfaction
  • Desire for a natural, long-term solution without medication or surgery
