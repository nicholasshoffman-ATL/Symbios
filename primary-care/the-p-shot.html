Restore Natural Male Function and Confidence

Erectile dysfunction (ED) can affect spontaneity, intimacy, and self-confidence. Whether it occurs occasionally or chronically, ED can interfere with your ability to enjoy a healthy sex life. At Symbios Health in Hilton Head Island, we offer The P-Shot® (Priapus Shot)—a safe, in-office treatment designed to naturally improve erectile function, enhance performance, and restore sexual vitality.

What is The P-Shot®?

The P-Shot® is a minimally invasive, platelet-rich plasma (PRP) treatment that uses your body’s own growth factors to stimulate tissue regeneration, increase blood flow, and improve penile function. Unlike medications, needles, or surgery, the P-Shot® addresses the underlying causes of ED for long-term results rather than simply masking symptoms.

  • Quick 15–20 minute in-office procedure
  • Painless, with no anesthesia required
  • No downtime—resume daily activities immediately
  • Natural, long-lasting results

How The P-Shot® Works

The procedure uses PRP derived from your blood, which is injected into targeted areas of the penis. PRP:

  • Stimulates new blood vessel growth
  • Improves vascular function
  • Promotes natural tissue regeneration

The treatment is performed in a private, comfortable environment. Most men notice improvements within weeks, with continued enhancement over time.

Who Can Benefit from The P-Shot®?

The P-Shot® is ideal for men experiencing:

  • Erectile dysfunction (occasional or chronic)
  • Difficulty achieving or maintaining erections
  • Reduced sexual confidence
  • Desire for a natural, long-term solution without medication or surgery
