Priapus Toxin® in Hilton Head Island, SC

A Breakthrough Treatment for Men’s Sexual Health

Erectile dysfunction (ED) is one of the most common concerns men face as they age. While medications like Viagra® can provide short-term solutions, they don’t always address the underlying causes of ED. At Symbios Health in Hilton Head Island, we offer an advanced treatment called Priapus Toxin®, a cutting-edge procedure that combines the power of Botox® with regenerative therapies to restore natural erectile function, improve blood flow, and enhance performance.

What is Priapus Toxin®?

Priapus Toxin® (PTX) is an innovative, in-office procedure that uses Botox® injections into penile tissue to relax smooth muscles, increase nitric oxide production, and boost blood flow. The result is improved erectile function, healthier penile tissue, and enhanced sexual responsiveness.

When combined with platelet-rich plasma (PRP) from the P-Shot®, studies suggest Priapus Toxin® may have a synergistic effect, improving both immediate erectile performance and long-term tissue regeneration.

How Priapus Toxin® Works

Botox is widely known for its ability to smooth facial lines, but when used in men’s sexual health, it offers powerful benefits:

  • Increases penile blood flow by relaxing smooth muscle tissue
  • Enhances nitric oxide production, essential for strong erections
  • Promotes new nerve and blood vessel growth
  • Stimulates natural tissue regeneration for long-term improvement

The procedure is quick, minimally invasive, and performed in the comfort of our Hilton Head Island office with no downtime.

Benefits of Priapus Toxin® for Erectile Dysfunction

Men who receive Priapus Toxin® may experience:

  • Stronger, longer-lasting erections
  • Improved sexual sensitivity and responsiveness
  • Greater confidence in the bedroom
  • Healthier penile tissue and potential length improvement
  • A natural, restorative option without dependency on medication
