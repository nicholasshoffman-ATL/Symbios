The P-Shot® (Priapus Shot)

The P-Shot® uses platelet-rich plasma (PRP) from your own blood to stimulate tissue regeneration, improve blood flow, and enhance penile function. Benefits include:

  • Stronger, firmer erections
  • Increased sensitivity and sexual pleasure
  • Improved stamina and performance
  • Clinically validated results lasting up to two years

Performed in-office, the P-Shot® is painless, requires no downtime, and provides natural, long-term results.

Learn About The P-Shot® (Priapus Shot)

Priapus Toxin®

Priapus Toxin® is an innovative treatment that uses Botox® injections in penile tissue to relax smooth muscles, increase blood flow, and improve nitric oxide production. When combined with the P-Shot®, Priapus Toxin® can:

  • Enhance erectile function and responsiveness
  • Promote nerve and blood vessel growth
  • Support healthier tissue and natural length
  • Improve confidence and sexual performance

This quick, minimally invasive procedure is performed in a private, comfortable setting with no downtime.
