Reclaim Your Confidence This Men’s Health Month with Advanced ED Solutions

Resources · Men's Health

June is here, and with it comes a special opportunity to celebrate Men’s Health Month and honor Father’s Day by prioritizing your health and happiness. At Symbios Health, we are committed to helping men – from fathers to sons to brothers and friends – regain their vitality, rebuild confidence, and strengthen relationships.

Erectile dysfunction (ED) is a challenge shared by more than 30 million men in the United States alone. It affects 50% of men over 40 and even 25% under 40. Despite its prevalence, many suffer silently, feeling the toll it takes on self-esteem and intimacy. The good news? ED is treatable. Causes such as diabetes, heart disease, stress, or low testosterone don’t have to define you. You have the power to take charge of your life, and we’re here to help.

Why Symbios Health Is Different

Led by Dr. Stephen Luther, Symbios Health focuses on solutions that address ED at its core – not just its symptoms. Dr. Luther is the only physician in Beaufort County certified in the Priapus Shot (P-Shot), making him a trusted expert in advanced, non-invasive ED treatments.

Here’s a closer look at how we help men reclaim their confidence and joy without the need for risky medications or invasive procedures:

Innovative ED Treatments at Symbios Health

Alma Duo

This cutting-edge, painless treatment uses focused ultrasound waves to improve blood flow and repair tissue. With no downtime, it’s a safe and effective way to enhance your performance naturally.

The Priapus Shot (P-Shot)

Harnessing the regenerative power of your own platelet-rich plasma (PRP), the P-Shot can improve erectile function and even reduce the curvature caused by Peyronie’s disease in 70-80% of cases. It’s a game-changer for men looking to restore intimacy.

Priapus Toxin (P-Tox Shot)

When paired with the P-Shot, the P-Tox Shot relaxes muscles and enhances circulation, amplifying the overall results. It’s an innovative way to address ED from multiple angles.

These treatments are designed to meet the needs of all men, including those with chronic conditions. Unlike common ED medications like Viagra, which may cause headaches, vision or hearing changes, or even heart problems, our therapies are safe and targeted. We treat the root causes of ED to help you achieve lasting results.
