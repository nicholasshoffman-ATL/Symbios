About Us

About Us

Who We Are

At Symbios® Health, we believe true wellness goes beyond just treating symptoms—it’s about building healthier habits that elevate your everyday life. Our unique, integrated health and wellness campus brings together primary care, fitness, nutrition, and aesthetics—all in one connected location—to support your health from every angle.

Whether you’re here for preventive care, personalized training, hormone optimization, skin rejuvenation, or nutritional support, our expert team collaborates to create a plan that’s tailored to you.

The Symbios Health campus is more than a place—it’s a community dedicated to personalized, holistic care. We focus on measurable results and sustainable change, guiding you through each stage of behavioral transformation—from awareness to lasting action.

Designed Around Proven Principles

Our Programs

Our programs are designed around proven principles of behavioral change:

Consciousness Raising

Helping you understand the why behind your habits.

Self-Reevaluation

Encouraging personal reflection and intrinsic motivation.

Commitment to Self

Supporting your decision to change with intention and clarity.

Behavioral Change & Accountability

Providing structure, guidance, and support as you take action.

New Identity & Lifestyle

Helping you internalize change so that healthy behaviors become part of who you are.

At every step, we meet you where you are—whether you’re saying “I want,” “I will,” “I did,” or “I am”—and help you move forward with confidence. We don’t just treat—we transform.

Our Approach

What’s In It for You

At Symbios® Health, we don’t just treat—we transform. Our one-of-a-kind campus offers everything you need to live healthy, fit, and beautiful for life, all in one place. Here’s what sets us apart:

Whole-Person Care with Purpose

We take a holistic approach that supports not just your physical health, but your personal growth and daily performance—so you can thrive, not just survive. Every human needs to sleep (rest), nourish, move, critically think, self-care, relate, and spiritually grow which we believe are the seven pillars to holistic health.

Integrated Services on Our One-of-a-Kind Campus

From primary care and fitness to nutrition, aesthetics, and hormone optimization, our experts collaborate seamlessly across specialties for truly comprehensive support.

Real Results, Backed by Data

Every program is personalized and driven by measurable outcomes across key metabolic, behavioral, and physical markers—so you can track your progress and feel the difference.

Meet the Experts

Our Leadership Team

Stephen S. Luther, MD

Founder

Dr. Stephen Luther is a board-certified internal medicine specialist with a focus on primary care. He earned his medical degree from Indiana University Medical School and completed his residency in internal medicine at Methodist Hospital in Indianapolis. Before relocating to Hilton Head Island in 2007, he practiced internal medicine and served as Director of Perioperative Medicine at Kendrick Memorial Hospital/St. Francis Hospital in Mooresville, Indiana. On Hilton Head, Dr. Luther has served as Medical Director of the Cardiac Rehab Program at Beaufort Memorial Hospital and is a member of the American College of Physicians. As founder of Symbios Health, he is dedicated to delivering comprehensive care that enhances wellness and improves quality of life.

Joe Kirchner

Chief Financial Officer

Joe Kirchner is a fractional CFO with over 30 years of experience in finance, accounting, and consulting. He works directly with business owners to manage cash flow, build budgets, and develop financial strategies aligned with their business goals.

Alison Haynes

Vice President of Operations

Alison Haynes is the Vice President of Operations at Symbios Health, bringing more than two decades of experience in patient care and healthcare management. She began her career as a medical assistant and advanced into leadership through a commitment to operational excellence and quality improvement. Alison leads a team dedicated to creating efficient, compassionate care settings that support both patients and staff.

Victoria Blintser

VP of Regenerative Health and Wellness

Victoria brings a thoughtful, client-centered approach to aesthetics and wellness. As Vice President of Regenerative Health and Wellness at Symbios Health, she helps lead the growth and development of ARIA and Symbios Fit, focusing on team empowerment, service excellence, and meaningful client experiences. She is passionate about building an environment where people feel welcome, respected, and well cared for throughout their journey.

Wade Welsh

VP of Human Performance

Wade Welsh is a board-certified orthopedic clinical specialist and Fellow of the American Academy of Orthopedic Manual Physical Therapists. At Symbios Physical Therapy, Wade provides specialized care for acute injuries, pre- and post-surgical rehabilitation, and chronic condition management.
