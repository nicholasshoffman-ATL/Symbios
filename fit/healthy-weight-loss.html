Fit Rx is More Than Weight Loss — A Complete Lifestyle Reset for Men

On our one-of-a-kind campus, we do more than help you lose weight. We provide the tools, support, and expert guidance to create lasting lifestyle changes that improve your energy, mobility, and overall well-being.

Personalized From the Start

To ensure your plan is safe, effective, and tailored to your unique goals, we begin with a comprehensive assessment of your:

  • Metabolism
  • Blood biomarkers
  • Nutrition
  • Movement patterns

Expert Team, Proven Approach

Your journey is guided by a collaborative team including:

  • Physician
  • Doctor of Physical Therapy
  • Certified Personal Trainers
  • Nurse Practitioners & Physician Assistants
  • Health Coach

What's Included in Your 12-Week Program

  • 3D Body Composition Scans via Styku – Track weight, body fat %, muscle mass, and progress
  • Heart Health Monitoring – Heart rate, recovery, and blood pressure checks
  • Monthly Nutritional Check-Ins – Custom, evidence-based nutrition planning
  • Functional Movement Assessments – Improve mobility, balance, and prevent injury
  • Monthly Healthcare Provider Consults – Ongoing medical oversight
  • Medication Management – GLP-1s and other options when appropriate (medication cost not included)
  • 20 Personal Training Sessions – Customized workouts for your level
  • Complimentary Gym Membership – Full access to our facility for 12 weeks
