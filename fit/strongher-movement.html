Women's Wellness & Strength

Women's Wellness & Strength in Hilton Head Island, SC

Introducing StrongHer – Strength, Balance, and Confidence for Women

StrongHer Movement is designed specifically for women at every stage of life. This 12-week program combines strength training, mobility work, and targeted hormonal support to help you move better, feel stronger, and thrive in daily life. Whether you're navigating perimenopause, post-pregnancy, or simply want to feel more empowered in your body, StrongHer promotes resilience from the inside out.

More Than a Workout — StrongHer is a Whole-Body Wellness Program

On our one-of-a-kind campus, StrongHer is more than a fitness plan. We provide a science-based, supportive program that improves strength, mobility, balance, and hormonal health for long-term wellness.

Personalized From the Start

To ensure your plan is safe, effective, and tailored to your unique needs and stage of life, we begin with a comprehensive assessment of your:

  • Body composition
  • Cardiovascular health
  • Movement patterns

Expert Team, Proven Approach

StrongHer is guided by a collaborative team including:

  • Healthcare providers
  • Certified personal trainers
  • Nutrition and wellness specialists

What's Included in Your 12-Week Program

  • 3D Body Composition Scan via Styku – Track weight, body fat %, muscle mass, and progress
  • Heart Health Monitoring – Baseline and follow-up checks for heart rate, recovery, and blood pressure
  • Monthly Nutritional Check-Ins – Personalized, evidence-based nutrition planning
  • Functional Movement Assessment – Improve mobility, balance, and reduce injury risk
  • Monthly Healthcare Provider Consults – Ongoing oversight and progress tracking
  • Hormonal Support Strategies – Exercise, nutrition, lifestyle, and medication guidance when appropriate (medication cost not included)
  • 20 Personal Training Sessions – One-on-one coaching tailored to your goals
  • Complimentary Gym Membership – Full access to our facility for 12 weeks
