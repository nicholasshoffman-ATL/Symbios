Our Services

What Our Comprehensive Assessments Include

Metabolic Evaluation

We analyze your metabolism to understand how your body processes energy, helping to design strategies that support weight management, energy levels, and overall wellness.

Blood Biomarker Testing

Our on-site testing evaluates key health indicators, including glucose, cholesterol, hormones, and other markers, allowing us to detect potential issues early and guide targeted interventions.

Nutritional Assessment

We review your diet and nutrient intake to ensure your body is getting the fuel it needs. Personalized recommendations help optimize energy, support healthy weight, and improve long-term health.

Movement & Functional Assessment

Our team evaluates posture, strength, flexibility, and movement patterns to identify areas of imbalance or limitation. This ensures any exercise or rehabilitation program is safe, effective, and tailored to your needs.
