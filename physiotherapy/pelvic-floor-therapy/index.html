Our Services

Pelvic Floor Therapy Services

We take a gentle, evidence-based approach to pelvic health, providing treatments such as:

  • Manual Therapy – Soft tissue techniques to release tight muscles and improve circulation.
  • Biofeedback Training – Guided techniques to help you activate and control pelvic muscles.
  • Core Strengthening Exercises – Targeted movements to stabilize the pelvis and support posture.
  • Postpartum Rehabilitation – Recovery and strengthening for women following childbirth.
  • Education & Lifestyle Guidance – Strategies to improve bladder habits, posture, and daily movement.

Each plan is personalized to your comfort level and designed to support long-term pelvic and core health.
