Our Services

Lymphedema Therapy Services

Our licensed physical therapists use evidence-based techniques to support circulation, relieve swelling, and promote healing. Your care plan may include:

  • Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD) – Gentle, specialized massage to stimulate lymph flow and reduce fluid buildup.
  • Compression Therapy – Use of bandages or garments to prevent swelling and maintain fluid balance.
  • Exercise and Movement Training – Guided exercises that encourage lymph flow and improve strength and flexibility.
  • Skin Care and Infection Prevention – Education on hygiene and skin health to reduce complications.
  • Patient Education – Guidance on self-management techniques, compression use, and long-term maintenance.

Each plan is tailored to your individual condition, medical history, and goals.
