Our Services

Specialized Physical Therapy Services

  • Lymphedema Management: Certified therapists provide comprehensive care to reduce swelling and improve circulation for lymphedema patients.
  • Pelvic Floor Therapy: Targeted treatments for pelvic floor dysfunction, including incontinence, pelvic pain, and post-partum recovery.
  • Vestibular Rehabilitation: Exercises and therapy to improve balance, reduce dizziness, and manage vestibular disorders effectively.
  • Oncology Rehabilitation: Pre- and post-cancer treatment therapy to help patients regain strength, mobility, and overall function.
